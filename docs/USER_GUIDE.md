# LessonForge User Guide

Welcome to LessonForge - an interactive lesson builder and learning platform designed for Christian education.

---

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Navigate to the LessonForge homepage
2. Click "Sign In" and enter your credentials
3. Demo accounts are available for testing:
   - **Teacher:** teacher@lessonforge.demo / password123
   - **Student:** student@lessonforge.demo / password123

### Creating an Account

1. Click "Create Account" on the login screen
2. Fill in your name, email, and password (minimum 8 characters)
3. Select your role (Student or Teacher)
4. Click "Create Account"

---

## Dashboard

The dashboard is your home base, showing:

### Daily Verse
Each day features a new scripture verse focused on learning, wisdom, or guidance. This provides encouragement and spiritual grounding for your educational journey.

### Learning Stats
Track your progress with:
- **Lessons Started** - Total lessons you've begun
- **Completed** - Lessons fully finished
- **Avg. Score** - Your average quiz performance
- **Time Learning** - Total time spent on lessons

### Recent Lessons
Quick access to lessons you've been working on, with progress indicators.

## For Teachers: Creating Lessons

### Step 1: Access the Lesson Builder
Click "Lesson Builder" in the sidebar or the "Create Lesson" button on the dashboard.

### Step 2: Add Lesson Details
Fill in the basic information:
- **Title** - A clear, descriptive name
- **Description** - What students will learn
- **Subject** - Mathematics, Science, English Language Arts, History, or Bible Studies
- **Grade Level** - K-1 through Grade 9-12

### Step 3: Add Content Blocks

Click the block type buttons to add content:

#### 📝 Text Block
Add written content with optional section titles. Use for explanations, instructions, or reading material.

#### 🧠 Quiz Block
Create multiple-choice questions:
1. Enter the question
2. Add 4 answer options
3. Select the correct answer (radio button)

#### 🎬 Video Block
Embed educational videos:
1. Enter a title
2. Paste the YouTube embed URL

#### 📖 Scripture Block
Include Bible verses with reflection:
1. Enter the reference (e.g., "John 3:16")
2. Type the verse text
3. Add a reflection question or application

### Step 4: Save or Publish
- **Save Draft** - Save for later editing
- **Publish Lesson** - Make available to students

## For Students: Taking Lessons

### Finding Lessons
1. Click "My Lessons" in the sidebar
2. Browse available lessons
3. Use the search bar to find specific topics
4. Filter by subject using the dropdown

### Taking a Lesson
1. Click on any lesson card to open it
2. Read through the content blocks
3. Watch embedded videos
4. Answer quiz questions by clicking your answer
5. Reflect on scripture passages

### Tracking Progress
Your progress is automatically saved as you complete each section. View your overall progress in the "Progress" section.

## Daily Verse Feature

Navigate to "Daily Verse" in the sidebar to:
- Read today's featured scripture
- Browse verses by theme (Learning, Wisdom, Diligence, etc.)
- Find inspiration for your studies

## Tips for Success

### For Teachers
- Start lessons with engaging questions or scenarios
- Break complex topics into smaller blocks
- Include a Scripture block to connect learning with faith
- Use quizzes to check understanding

### For Students
- Take your time with each block
- Review quiz answers to understand mistakes
- Reflect on how the daily verse applies to your learning
- Track your progress regularly
